Daniel Kim

Portfolio Theme: Finding Refuge

Refuge isn't just limited to the most comfortable of bedrooms, or the most welcoming houses, or even our most frequented local restaurants. It finds form in the people we're surrounded by, as well as the memories and connections we create with our environment as a whole. Often, while confined to a pattern of always looking to the future, we miss out on the past and present. But through my artworks, I hope to take time to reflect on the homes I've found and put a pause on life to appreciate my surroundings. Observing and visually representing the thoughts, experiences, and places I've discovered throughout childhood and adolescence is how I find my refuge.
